About Us

Mindteck is a global technology solutions company founded in 1991, with its headquarters in Bangalore, India. The company provides product engineering solutions and IT services to various clients, including Fortune 100 companies, universities, and government agencies. Mindteck is publicly traded on the Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE 517344) and the National Stock Exchange (NSE Mindteck).
